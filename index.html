Random Classical Music Generator

Welcome, welcome! Listening to classical music is like reading a book. Starting an hour-and-a-half concerto could be as daunting as embarking on a 500-page book. This tool recommends shorter pieces to get you started on this journey of discovering classical music.


Each page will have a link to the video, other arrangements, and some background information. Some will also have examples of how one may interpret the piece. This varies from person to person. With more listening practice you'll be able to feel more and interpret things in your own way.


If you're interested in learning more about music compositions, Inside the Score is a good place to start. Pre-requisite musical background or knowledge is not required - but the curiosity and eagerness to learn is rather necessary.


Happy listening :)
